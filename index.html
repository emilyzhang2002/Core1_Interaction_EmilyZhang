Hello :-)

Hi, my name is Jinyi Zhang ( Emily ). I’m a student from Parsons School of Design major in Communication design.

This is my portfolio. I like drawing and installation art very much, and I like to express my ideas in those ways. I was born in a happy family with a strong artistic atmosphere. My mother is a fine art teacher. I still remember the first time I learned to use a pencil to draw something when I was only four years old. During my primary school years, I participated in many painting competitions and gained a series of prizes. Many of my classmates began to collect my works because they thought my paintings were beautiful and worth collecting. Even at such a young age, I initiated the idea regarding my future career, to design beautiful visual art works and make our world more colorful.
